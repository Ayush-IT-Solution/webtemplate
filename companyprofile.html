About Us Company Profile

company profile

Sufi international, based in New Delhi, introduces itself as one of the India's leading Manpower Recruitment Agency for Domestic and overseas countries, like Middle East, South-East Asia, Africa, Europe, USA, UK, Australia and New Zealand. We are duly approved and existing under and by virtue of the laws of the Indian Govt. We are established in the field of recruitment & manpower providers since 2000. During all these years we have successfully catered and provided requisite manpower requirement of various Companies in India and Overseas countries to their entire satisfaction since we are a one- source multi-resources organization. We have provided our services to the critical core industries i.e. Oil & Gas (Petrochemical, Refinery, Fertilizer, Power plants etc.), Merchant Navy, Hotel/Restaurant, Infrastructure Development, IT, Telecom, Banking, FMCG, Consumer Goods and Health Care. Our Modus Operandi / Core strength is our well trained / experienced staff who are specialized in talent hunt . The Sufi International team is comprised of professionals from all corners of the recruiting industries in the field of Oil and Gas, Entertainment, Media, Publishing, Call Centers , Banks, Petrochemicals, Building Construction, Merchant Navy, IT Professionals, Hotel / Restaurant Staff and many more.
