Q: 4. What is Sufi International's Ten Point Plan as to how they would satisfy our recruitment requirement.?

Ans : Please find below our ten point plan of as to how we would satisfy your recruitment requirement. The under mentioned process are undertaken simultaneously:
i) Databank (Immediately on receipt of the requirement) Sufi International has an extensive computerized databank. Basically highly Skilled & Technically qualified Professional categories required are sourced from our database.
ii) Job Postings in Job portals (the day of receipt of requirements) We post the requirements on our Job portal (www.Sufi Internationaloverseas.com) and also have contractual access to 3 leading premier jobsites i.e. Naukri.com, Timesjobs.com & Monster.com of India which contains large database for all industries meeting the International Standard.
iii) Headhunting Activity (After 3 days of receipt of the requirements) We also conduct headhunting in demographic areas where certain specialized categories are available. This activity is an added advantage in sourcing required categories from Specific Industries.
iv) Advertising (After studying the requirement the agency advises the client for releasing the Advertisement) The required categories are advertised in leading English language and in select regional language newspapers which covers the cities which target the candidates as per the industry. We plan our advertisement schedules based on the categories required, and their availability in different parts of India. We also advertise in Assignment Abroad Times, a niche job search periodical, so that we reach out to the specific candidates targeting Specific parts of India.
v) Personal interview & Screening (The process starts after receiving the response from Databank, Job posting Advertising & Headhunting) The cv's sourced from the above process are been screened and short-listed by our highly experienced and technically qualified Recruitment Managers on the basis of the job description provided by our client. The semi-skilled categories are also subjected to a trade test as per their specialization. The overall grading of candidates is carried out based on their professional skills, previous experience, general knowledge, personality and adaptability for work environment. The candidate's shortlisted by us is then presented to the client's representative for the final selection.
vi) Planning the Recruitment Trip for Client We prepare the itinerary for our clients to visit different cities in India for final interviews which shall enable us to fulfill the requirement of our clients.
vii) Hotel Bookings We also assist our clients to do the hotel bookings on their behalf in different cities where the interviews are to be conducted.
viii) Pre-Screening by our Recruitment Managers: Our Technically qualified Recruitment Managers travel to different cities for pre-screening & shortlisting the candidates before the final interviews. Also our Managers travel with the delegates to different cities to provide them necessary assistance required at the time of the interviews.
ix) Interview arrangements and Facilities: We have a huge spacious office constituting of 5000 odd Sq. feet. with 10 specialized air-conditioned cabins to conduct interviews along with Computers, broadband connection, Intercom and Secretarial Assistance. Sufi International uses Windows XP as a standardized software. The computer are safe guarded with Symantec Norton Antivirus, we maintain a weekly checkup of Antivirus. We use email, faxes, STD, ISD & Video Conferencing facilities for communication.
x) To make the entire Recruitment Trip successful we believe in strong communication and co-ordination with our clients and pre-screened candidates.
