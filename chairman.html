About Us Chairman's Corner

Jeetendra Singh, CEO Sufi International

Chairman's Message

Born in 1976, Jeetendra Singh began his career in the recruiting and staffing industry over 20 years ago. Along with his dedication and commitment towards his work, he quickly grasps the needs and requirements of his clients and candidates. He has a positive approach & great talent which makes him attractive enough to provide unique recruitment services & Manpower services to the people for both permanent and locum positions. He has good amount of knowledge on how interview should be conducted as he has successfully catered and provided requisite manpower requirement to a number of companies in India, Middle East and also in the overseas countries. He holds a good command over recruiting people in various fields.

Carrying extensive knowledge of this industry based on 24 years of experience, he genuinely enjoys working one on one with his clients and candidates. Jeetendra Singh, being the CEO of Sufi International, is an International traveler and has also trained almost more than 2 lacks people in the Middle East, South-East Asia, Africa, Europe, USA, UK, Australia and New Zealand. He builds his database appreciably and is literally very committed towards bringing job opportunities to the elite candidates. He provides placement services in an efficient, effective and timely manner which makes him more passionate in accomplishing his client’s expectations and assisting them with the talent acquisition. A good recruitment process can help in reducing the time involved in searching, interviewing, recruiting and training. It can also help in streamlining the procedures and can help you in making your search more effective and efficient for right candidates. This is what makes him unique from other recruiters. Jeetendra Singh has an excellent understanding of the needs and requirements of his clients or candidates which helps in to find the best talent for them. He is much passionate in his goal of surpassing his clients and candidates expectations and assists them in the talent acquisition.

He has an impressive track record of solidifying high-profile clients and candidates. His ultimate goal is to provide superior recruiting services to his clients and candidates so that they can work towards betterment and produce more output. He also leads his team by example with connecting qualified candidates with worth able positions. He believes that everyone deserves to be happy in their workplace which then allows them to be more productive in their workplace. This thought of him makes him unique from each and every recruiter of the world. And this thought of him helps in to find best talent for his clients and candidates.

Sufi international, based in New Delhi, introduces itself as one of the India's leading Manpower Recruitment Agency for Domestic and overseas countries, like Middle East, South-East Asia, Africa, Europe, USA, UK, Australia and New Zealand.
